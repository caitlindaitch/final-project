Zentangle (Zen/taNGɡəl)
Noun

A form of black-and-white abstract art based on precise and repetitive patterns. This style of art can range from simple doodles to complex and intricate form of art. Zentangles are composed of structured patterns called "tangles" and often are unplanned in nature--the results tend to surprise even the artists themselves.

Typically, zentangles are created primarily or exclusively using an ink pen. Cult favorite (for good reason) is the Sakura Pigma Micron Pen.
